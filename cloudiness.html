Latitude vs Cloudiness


The cloudiness scatter plot shows that a broad range of values are present at all latitudes, and cloudiness does not appear to change with changing latitude.

This is confirmed by the Pearson's correlation coefficients for the southern and northern hemispheres which were very low at 0.027 and 0.143 respeciviely
